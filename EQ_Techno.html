UX Designer

eQ Technologic is seeking a User Experience Engineer, responsible for bringing the user to the forefront of the software development process. This role requires a strong understanding of user experience best practices and hands on experience with interaction design, visual design, information architecture, user research and UX strategy.

This is an exciting opportunity for the right individual to advocate to a cross functional team the needs of the user. You will be working across disciplines, organizational and geographical boundaries and with customers from diverse industries to rapidly develop world-class enterprise software. Your challenge will be to take large, powerful and complex applications and transform them into the simple, intuitive and compelling experience that modern user expectations demand, while preserving their sophisticated capabilities. You will be working in a fast-paced, agile environment where you will have to keep pace with the rapidly evolving technologies and standards. The ideal candidate is driven by the desire to be a user advocate and is passionate about creating products that are intuitive, satisfying, and easy to learn.

Job Description: The User Experience Engineer is responsible for working with the Product Owner and our Customer to extract features that will meet user needs and improve business results. You will wear many hats and be asked to develop a variety of UX deliverables from personas to mock-ups
