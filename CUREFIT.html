Health and Fitness Industry

CureFit is a health and fitness company offering digital and offline experiences across fitness, nutrition, and mental With the aim to make fitness fun and easy, CureFit gives workouts a whole new meaning with a range of trainer-led, group workout classes. It makes workouts fun, daily food is healthy and tasty, mental fitness easy withyoga and meditation, and medical and lifestyle care hassle-free. Co-founded by Mukesh Bansal and Ankit in Nagori 2016, the company is headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka.
