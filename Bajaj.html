Skills /Eligibility

    GTE period is for 1 year from Date of Joining

    Bajaj Auto provides accommodation to Graduate Engineer Trainees for the first 2 years. In locations where such accommodation is not provided, GTEs would receive INR 8000/- p.m. towards Stay expenses for first 2 years

    Annual increments at Bajaj Auto are calculated on amount mentioned in point no. C i.e. CTC (Fixed Gross + Maximum Performance Reward)

    The Maximum Performance Reward is an annual payment. Employees are ranked in 6 different ranking clusters based on their performance and other parameters. As per the current policy, employees ranked in top 4 ranking clusters (75%) are eligible for Performance Reward. The reward percentage varies based on the ranking of the individual.
